Poema

Las Vegas

Por M. S. M.

"In the desert’s heart, a city gleams,
Where fortune whispers in the dreams.
Neon lights in the velvet night,
A gambler’s hope, a lover’s flight.

Cards are shuffled, dice are cast,
In the echoes of laughter, memories last.
The Strip unfolds, a dazzling show,
Where the sands of time refuse to slow.

In glitzy halls, ambitions rise,
Under the watch of a million eyes.
Each moment crafted, each deal made,
In this oasis, where fortunes fade.

Beyond the glamour, a tale untold,
Of hearts that shine and souls that fold.
Las Vegas, a mirage so bright,
A paradox wrapped in the desert night."
